Last ride

Justice League: Last Ride (Volume 1) is a limited series, published by DC Comics. It began publication in 2021. It follows the Justice League in a possible future as they prepare for one last adventure together.



Future state

"Future State" is a comic book storyline published by DC Comics in January and February 2021,[a] consisting of multiple limited series released in place of DC's regular ongoing series during those months. The event is set in the aftermath of the "Dark Nights: Death Metal" storyline, and takes place in a "possible future" of the DC Universe.The conclusion of the event leads into DC's Infinite Frontier relaunch



Dark

The Justice League Dark, or JLD, is a fictional superhero team appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. First appearing in Justice League Dark #1 (September 2011), the Justice League Dark originally featured John Constantine, Madame Xanadu, Deadman, Shade: The Changing Man, and Zatanna, eventually being joined by prominent members such as Swamp Thing and Wonder Woman.
